What you need to qualify:

Employed in Government Sector Salary Transferred to SNB:

  • Income:
    • Minimum of SAR 2,500
  • Years on Job:
    • Minimum of 1 month
  • Age in Hijri:
    • Minimum of 18 years
  • Required Documents:
    • Car Price Quotation
    • Original and Copy of Valid ID/Iqama
    • Original and Copy of Valid Driver's License
    • Employment Letter

Easy steps to financing your car:

  1. Find your car
    • Visit a dealership and choose the car you want. Once you’ve decided, record the car’s details and the quote.
  2. Get pre-approval From SNB
    • Take the quote to the SNB representative at the dealership or visit an SNB finance center. Fill in the application forms and get an instant pre-approval decision.
  3. Get final approval
    • You’ll get an SMS with the final approval decision within two business days. Once you’ve received approval, your new car will be delivered within ten business days.
