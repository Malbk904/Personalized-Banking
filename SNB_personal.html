What you need to qualify:

Resident Status:

Open to Saudis and non-Saudis.

Salary Transfer:

Government or private sector employees who receive their salary through the Sarie system into their SNB account.

Minimun monthly income:

  • Saudis= SAR 3,000
  • Non-Saudis - SAR 4,000

Minimum employment term:

  • One month for Saudis in the government sector.
  • Three months for Saudis in the big 5, and non-Saudis in the government sector and big private companies.
  • One year for Saudis and non-Saudis in other private sector companies.

Age:

  • Minimum age:
    • Saudis – 18 years
    • Non-Saudis – 22 years
  • Maximum age:
    • Must be less than 60 at the date of the final installment.
    • Retirees must be less than 75 at the date of the final installment.
